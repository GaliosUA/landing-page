NEC PC-98 Introduction

The PC-9800 series (Japanese: PC-9800シリーズ, Hepburn: Pī Shī Kyūsen Happyaku Shirīzu), commonly shortened to PC-98 or 98 (キューハチ, Kyū-hachi), is a lineup of Japanese 16-bit and 32-bit personal computers manufactured by NEC from 1982 to 2000. It is widely known as a treasure trove of many Japanese-only adventure and roleplaying games that boast superior graphics compared to their Western contemporaries.
